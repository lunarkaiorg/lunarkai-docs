The AI Assistant is the primary interface of Lunark AI, designed to understand and process natural language commands for blockchain interactions. It combines advanced language understanding with blockchain expertise to provide a seamless user experience.

## Natural Language Processing

The AI Assistant understands user intentions through natural conversation. Whether you're looking to make a simple transfer or engage in complex DeFi operations, you can express your goals in plain language.

Example interactions with AI Assistant:

```javascript
User: "What's the current gas price on Ethereum?"
Lunark: "Current gas fees on Ethereum are averaging 25 gwei ($2.50)."

User: "Send 1 ETH to John"
Lunark: "I've prepared a transaction to send 1 ETH to John's address. The current gas fee is approximately $2.50. [Execute Transfer]"

User: "What's the best route to swap USDT for ETH?"
Lunark: "I've found the most efficient route through Uniswap V3, which will give you 0.425 ETH for your 1000 USDT. [Execute Swap]"
```

## Contextual Understanding

Our AI Assistant maintains conversation context, remembering relevant details from previous interactions to provide more accurate and personalized responses. This means you don't need to repeat information or specify details that can be inferred from context.

## Network Management

The AI Assistant automatically detects your current network and helps manage network switches when needed. If a requested operation requires a different network, the Assistant will guide you through the process with clear instructions.

```javascript
// Network Management Example
User: "Swap USDT for MATIC"
Lunark: "I see you're on Ethereum network, but MATIC swaps are more efficient on Polygon. Would you like to switch networks? [Switch to Polygon]"

User: "Yes, switch to Polygon"
Lunark: "Network switch completed. I can now help you swap USDT for MATIC on Polygon with lower gas fees."
```
