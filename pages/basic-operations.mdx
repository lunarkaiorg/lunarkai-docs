## Essential Commands

Here's how to perform basic blockchain operations through natural language:

Basic balance and price checks:

```javascript
User: "What's my ETH balance?"
Lunark: "You have 2.5 ETH (≈ $5,000) in your wallet"

User: "Show me USDT price"
Lunark: "Current USDT prices:
ETH: 0.000425 (≈ $1.00)
[Set Price Alert] [Trade USDT]"
```

```javascript
User: "Check my tokens"

- 2.5 ETH ($5,000)

- 500 MATIC ($500)
[View All] [Track Portfolio]"
```
## Network Interactions

Lunark AI automatically detects your current network and helps you navigate between different blockchains. The system ensures you're always on the right network for your intended operation and helps manage network switching when needed.

Network management:

```javascript

User: "Switch to Polygon"
Lunark: "Preparing to switch networks:
Current: Ethereum → Target: Polygon
[Confirm Network Switch]"
```

```javascript

- 500 MATIC
- 200 USDT
[View All] [Bridge Assets]"
```
