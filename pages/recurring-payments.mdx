Recurring Payments will enable automated, scheduled blockchain transactions. This planned feature will allow users to set up regular payments, DeFi interactions, and automated trading strategies through simple natural language commands.

## Payment Automation

Setting up recurring payments will be as simple as describing your needs. The system will handle scheduling, gas optimization, and transaction execution while keeping you informed of all activities. You'll be able to manage your recurring transactions with complete visibility and control over the process.

Setting up recurring payments:

```javascript
User: "Set up weekly payment of 100 USDT to John"
Lunark: "I'll help schedule a recurring payment:
When: Every Monday
Amount: 100 USDT
Recipient: John
First payment: Next Monday
[Confirm Schedule]"
```

```javascript
User: "Schedule monthly ETH DCA"
Lunark: "Setting up monthly DCA for ETH:
Amount: $500 worth of ETH
Timing: 1st of each month
Gas limit: 50 gwei max
[Start DCA Plan]"
```

## Smart Scheduling

The system will intelligently manage your recurring transactions by monitoring network conditions, optimizing gas costs, and ensuring reliable execution. Each scheduled transaction will be executed at the most advantageous time within your specified parameters.

Schedule management:

```javascript
User: "Show my recurring payments"
Lunark: "Active recurring transactions:
1. Weekly USDT to John
   Next payment: Monday
2. Monthly ETH DCA
   Next execution: Feb 1st
[Manage Schedules]"
```

```javascript
User: "Pause USDT payment to John"
Lunark: "Weekly payment to John paused.
Resume anytime. Next scheduled payment
will be skipped until you resume.
[Resume] [Edit Schedule]"
```

## Transaction Management

Managing your recurring payments will be straightforward and flexible. You'll have full control over your scheduled transactions with the ability to modify, pause, or cancel them at any time. The system will provide detailed tracking of all recurring payment activities and notify you of important updates or needed actions.

## Customization Options

The recurring payments system will adapt to your specific needs with customizable parameters for timing, amounts, and conditions. You'll be able to set up complex schedules, define execution conditions, and establish automated responses to market conditions.

Advanced scheduling:

```javascript
User: "Buy ETH weekly when gas is low"
Lunark: "Creating smart recurring buy:
Amount: Your specified ETH amount
Condition: Gas below 30 gwei
Frequency: Weekly window
[Confirm Setup]"
```

```javascript
User: "Set up auto-compound for staking rewards"

Protocol: Aave ETH staking
Frequency: Daily check

[Enable Auto-compound]"
```
