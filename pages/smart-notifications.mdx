Smart Notifications will serve as your personalized blockchain alert system. This upcoming feature will keep you informed about important events, market conditions, and transaction status updates through intelligent, context-aware notifications.

## Personalized Alerts

The notification system will learn from your activities and preferences to deliver relevant updates. You'll receive timely alerts about transaction completions, price movements, gas conditions, and important protocol updates that matter to your specific needs.

Setting up notifications:

```javascript 
User: "Alert me when ETH drops below $2000"
Lunark: "Price alert set:
Target: ETH below $2000
Current price: $2,350
You'll be notified when triggered
[Manage Alerts]"
```

```javascript 
User: "Notify me about high gas"
Lunark: "Gas price alert set:
Threshold: Above 50 gwei
Current gas: 25 gwei
[Edit Threshold]"
```

## Market Intelligence

Our smart notification system will monitor market conditions across various platforms and networks. You'll receive intelligent alerts about significant market movements, opportunities, and potential risks that align with your portfolio and trading patterns.

Market monitoring:

```javascript

```

## Transaction Monitoring

The system will provide comprehensive transaction monitoring across all your blockchain activities. You'll receive real-time updates about transaction status, confirmation times, and any required actions, ensuring you stay informed about your ongoing operations.

## Security Alerts

Security will remain a top priority with intelligent monitoring of your wallet activities. The system will alert you to unusual transactions, significant withdrawals, and potential security concerns, helping you maintain the safety of your assets.

Security notifications:

```javascript 
User: "Enable security alerts"
Lunark: "Security monitoring active:
- Large transfers
- Contract interactions
- Network activity changes
[Adjust Security Level]"
```

```javascript 
User: "Show my active alerts"
Lunark: "Current alert settings:
1. ETH price < $2000
2. High gas alerts
3. Staking rewards
4. Security monitoring
[Manage All Alerts]"
```
